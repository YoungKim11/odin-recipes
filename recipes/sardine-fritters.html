Sardine Fritters

Description

Beloved dish in Jamaica this crispy, savoury Sardine Fritters will make your mouth WATER. Enjoy it with a delicious garlic cilantro dipping sauce.

Made with sardine, tomato, onion, and seasonings

Ingredients

  • All-purpose Flour(non-bleached)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Tumeric
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Sardines
  • Oil
  • Garlic
  • Mayo
  • Cilantro

Steps

Sardine Fritters

  1. Mix Flour with Water
  2. Add Pepper and Tumeric to Batter
  3. Prep Vegetables
  4. Mix veggies, Sardines, and Batter together
  5. Fry Batter mix over hot Pan with Sardine Oil and regular oil

Garlic Cilantro Mayo Sauce

  1. Add mayo to mixing bowl
  2. Add chopped garlic and cilantro to mixing bowl
  3. Mix together well
  4. And serve
